Diaspora Entrepreneur Solutions

More than 2 million Zimbabweans call the diaspora home, with nearly 90% maintaining strong connections to their families, friends, and businesses back in Zimbabwe. We understand these unique ties and have crafted specialized solutions to bridge the distance.

Omasters Accountants has leveraged its extensive expertise and experience to develop a comprehensive package specifically tailored for diaspora entrepreneurs who want real-time visibility into their Zimbabwe-based business performance.

Our cloud accounting solutions empower you to monitor your business performance anytime, anywhere in the world. We ensure real-time processing of all paperwork and conduct daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly audits of your business operations, giving you complete peace of mind.

As Zimbabwe modernizes its statutory compliance requirements, navigating these changes from abroad can be challenging. Omasters Accountants assists diaspora entrepreneurs with tax compliance, payroll management, business registrations, and annual reviews, ensuring you remain fully compliant.
